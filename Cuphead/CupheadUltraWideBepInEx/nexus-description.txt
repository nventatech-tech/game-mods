=== DESCRIPTION (Nexus "Description" field, BBCode) ===
[size=4][b]Cuphead UltraWide[/b][/size]

Renders the game at your monitor's aspect ratio instead of forcing 16:9. Removes the black side bars (pillarbox) on ultrawide monitors. Nothing is stretched - the camera simply shows more of the scene, up to 21:9 (2.39:1).

[size=3][b]How it works[/b][/size]
The mod patches the game's camera code, replacing the hardcoded 16:9 aspect constants with your monitor's real aspect ratio, and keeps every camera rendering full screen. Aspect is detected automatically and clamped between 16:9 and 2.39:1.

[size=3][b]📦 Installation[/b][/size]
[list=1]
[*]Install BepInEx 5 in the game folder and run the game once
[*]Extract the archive into the game folder (or install with Vortex). The file lands in:
[code]Cuphead/BepInEx/plugins/CupheadUltraWideBepInEx.dll[/code]
[*]Play
[/list]

[size=3][b]Notes[/b][/size]
[list]
[*]Set the game's Overscan option to 0 (the mod also forces this)
[*]Some scripted scenes were designed for 16:9, so you may occasionally see the edge of a background
[*]If the screen still shows black bars, check [b]BepInEx/LogOutput.log[/b] - the mod logs every camera patch it applies - and post it in the comments
[/list]
