=== MOD NAME (Nexus "Name" field) ===
Give Me Everything

=== SHORT SUMMARY (Nexus "Summary" field, max 250 chars) ===
In-game cheat panel for CET. Money, components, XP, damage/XP multipliers, set level, every iconic weapon, cyberware, quickhacks and clothing, heal, ammo, NCPD control, romances, vehicles. One GIVE ME EVERYTHING button. Auto EN/PT-BR.

=== DESCRIPTION (Nexus "Description" field, BBCode) ===
[size=4][b]Give Me Everything[/b][/size]

In-game panel for [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/cyberpunk2077/mods/107]Cyber Engine Tweaks[/url]. Open the CET overlay and click what you want — no console commands. Every resource can be [b]added or removed[/b] (minus buttons), so it also works to balance a save. Six tabs: [b]Items[/b], [b]Consumables[/b], [b]Progression[/b], [b]Weapons[/b], [b]Gear[/b] and [b]Unlocks[/b], with a custom cyberpunk look.

[size=3][b]🎒 Items tab[/b][/size]
[list]
[*][b]GIVE ME EVERYTHING[/b] — one button: 10M eddies, all components, consumables, ammo and every weapon and gear piece below
[*][b]Custom amount[/b] — type any quantity and every row gets an extra button with it
[*][b]Money[/b] — add/remove 100K, 1M or 10M eddies
[*][b]Crafting / quickhack components[/b] — add/remove per tier
[*][b]Cyberware capacity shards[/b]
[/list]

[size=3][b]💊 Consumables tab (new in 1.3.0)[/b][/size]
[list]
[*][b]Healing[/b] — MaxDoc, Bounce Back and a Full Heal button (health, stamina and RAM to 100%)
[*][b]Grenades[/b] — Frag, EMP, Incendiary, Flash, Recon, Smoke, Biohazard and Ozob's Nose
[*][b]Ammo[/b] — all four ammo types
[/list]

[size=3][b]📈 Progression tab[/b][/size]
[list]
[*][b]Points[/b] — Perk, Attribute and Relic points (±1 / ±10), plus the Perk reset shard (respec item)
[*][b]XP[/b] — Level, Street Cred and all five skills (+1000 / +10000)
[*][b]Set level directly (new)[/b] — type the target level and hit Set: Level (up to 60), Street Cred (50), every skill (60)
[*][b]XP multiplier[/b] — 1x–10x slider, multiplies all XP you earn. Saved between sessions
[*][b]Damage multiplier (new)[/b] — 1x–10x slider, multiplies all damage you deal, quickhacks included. Saved between sessions, reapplies on load
[/list]

[size=3][b]🔫 Weapons tab[/b][/size]
[list]
[*]Every [b]iconic weapon[/b] — base game and Phantom Liberty — grouped by class, with a [b]search box[/b]
[*]"Owned" detection avoids duplicates; [b]Give all[/b] per class
[*]Items unavailable in your install (Phantom Liberty) are hidden automatically
[/list]

[size=3][b]🦾 Gear tab (new in 1.3.0)[/b][/size]
[list]
[*][b]Quickhacks[/b] — all 17 combat/control/covert/ultimate hacks at max tier
[*][b]Cyberware[/b] — iconic and top-tier: Sandevistans (Apogee, Falcon, Warp Dancer…), Berserks, all five best cyberdecks, MaxTac Mantis Blades, Phantom Liberty iconics (Quantum Tuner, Axolotl, Chrome Compressor…)
[*][b]Clothing[/b] — full Johnny Silverhand set, Aldecaldos jacket, David's jacket (Edgerunners) and more
[*]Same search, Owned detection and Give all as the Weapons tab
[/list]

[size=3][b]🔓 Unlocks tab[/b][/size]
[list]
[*][b]Romances[/b] — flag Judy, Panam, River or Kerry as romanceable, regardless of V's gender. Opens the door for scenes that haven't happened yet — it doesn't replay a failed scene
[*][b]Vehicles[/b] — unlock every purchasable vehicle for free
[*][b]NCPD (new)[/b] — clear your wanted level, or disable the police entirely until the game restarts
[/list]

[size=3][b]⭐ Everywhere[/b][/size]
[list]
[*][b]On-screen notifications[/b] — every action shows a short in-game message
[*][b]Hotkeys[/b] (CET > Bindings) — add $1,000,000, full heal, refill ammo, clear wanted level
[*][b]Language[/b] — follows your game language automatically: Portuguese (pt-BR) or English
[/list]

[size=3][b]📖 How to use[/b][/size]
Open the CET overlay (default: the key you chose on CET's first launch). The [b]Give Me Everything[/b] window shows up next to the console. Click buttons. Close the overlay and keep playing.

[size=3][b]📦 Installation[/b][/size]
Extract the archive into the game's root folder (the one that contains [i]bin[/i], [i]archive[/i], [i]r6[/i]...), or install with Vortex / Mod Organizer 2. The file lands in:
[code]bin/x64/plugins/cyber_engine_tweaks/mods/GiveMeEverything/init.lua[/code]

[size=3][b]📋 Requirements[/b][/size]
[list]
[*][url=https://www.nexusmods.com/cyberpunk2077/mods/107]Cyber Engine Tweaks[/url] (latest version for your game patch)
[/list]

[size=3][b]✅ Compatibility[/b][/size]
Game version 2.x. Item IDs are resolved at load time — if an item doesn't exist in your game version, its row simply doesn't appear (check [i]GiveMeEverything.log[/i] in the mod folder). No files are patched; safe to add or remove at any time. Works on Windows and Linux/Proton.

[size=3][b]🗑️ Uninstall[/b][/size]
Delete [code]bin/x64/plugins/cyber_engine_tweaks/mods/GiveMeEverything[/code]. Nothing else is touched.
